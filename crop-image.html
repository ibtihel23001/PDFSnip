FAQ

Common Questions

How do I crop to an exact size? +
After dragging a crop selection, type the exact pixel dimensions in the W × H fields on the right panel. The selection will update instantly. You can also use the aspect ratio presets to lock proportions while dragging.
Is my image uploaded anywhere? +
No. Everything runs locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your image never leaves your device.
What image formats are supported? +
You can load JPG, PNG, and WebP images. The output can be saved as JPG (smallest file), PNG (lossless, supports transparency), or WebP (modern, smallest file size).
Can I crop a PNG with transparency? +
Yes. If you select PNG as the output format, transparency in the original image is preserved in the cropped result. Choosing JPG or WebP will flatten transparency to a white background.
