FAQ

Questions Answered

Is PDFSnip really free? +
Yes — completely free with no hidden costs. We don't add watermarks, we don't limit the number of conversions, and we don't require an account. Our tools are funded by minimal, non-intrusive ads on the page.
Are my files secure? +
Absolutely. All file transfers are encrypted via HTTPS. Your files are processed on secure servers and automatically deleted after 1 hour. We never read, share, or store the contents of your documents.
What is the maximum file size? +
We support files up to 100MB for PDF tools and up to 50MB for image tools. If you need to work with larger files, you can split them first using our Split PDF tool, then process each part.
Do I need to install anything? +
No installation required. PDFSnip runs entirely in your browser. Just visit the site, upload your file, and download the result. Works on Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, and Android.
What image quality does PDF to JPG produce? +
Our PDF to JPG tool converts at up to 300 DPI by default, producing sharp, print-ready images. You can also choose between 72, 150, and 300 DPI depending on your use case.
How long does conversion take? +
Most files convert in 2–5 seconds. Larger or more complex documents may take up to 30 seconds. Our servers are optimized for speed and handle thousands of conversions per minute.
