FAQ

Common Questions

Is my PDF uploaded to a server? +
No. Everything runs in your browser using PDF.js. Your file never leaves your device.
Why does conversion take a moment? +
Each page is rendered on a canvas element by your CPU. Larger or more complex PDFs take a few seconds per page — this is normal.
What DPI should I pick? +
72 DPI for quick previews, 150 DPI for screen/sharing (default), 300 DPI for print-quality output.
Can I convert a password-protected PDF? +
Not currently. Please remove the password first in Adobe Acrobat or another tool, then convert here.
