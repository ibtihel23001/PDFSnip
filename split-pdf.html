FAQ

Common Questions

Is my PDF uploaded to a server? +
No. All splitting is done using pdf-lib, a JavaScript library running entirely in your browser. Your file never leaves your device.
What is the difference between the split modes? +
"Every Page" creates one PDF per page. "Page Ranges" lets you group pages into custom chunks (e.g. 1-3, 4-6). "Extract Pages" pulls specific pages into one single output PDF.
Will fonts and images be preserved? +
Yes. pdf-lib copies pages exactly as-is, preserving all fonts, images, and formatting from the original PDF.
How do I split a large PDF quickly? +
Use "Every Page" to split automatically, then download all at once as a ZIP file. This is the fastest way to break up a multi-page document.
