FAQ

Compress JPG — Common Questions

How does JPG compression work? +
JPG uses lossy compression — it selectively discards image data that is less visible to the human eye. Our tool balances file size reduction with visual quality to give you the best possible result.
Will there be quality loss? +
All JPG compression involves some quality trade-off. At 80–90% quality settings, the difference is virtually invisible. Our Auto mode intelligently chooses the right level for each image.
Can I compress multiple JPGs at once? +
Yes! Upload multiple JPG files in one go and compress them all simultaneously. Download each individually or as a single ZIP archive.
Does it work with JPEG files too? +
JPG and JPEG are the same format — just different file extensions. Our compressor accepts both .jpg and .jpeg files.
