FAQ

Common Questions

How many PDFs can I merge? +
There is no hard limit. You can merge dozens of PDFs. Keep in mind that very large merges may take longer since all processing happens in your browser.
Can I change the order before merging? +
Yes! After uploading, drag and drop the file rows to reorder them. Files are merged in the order they appear in the list.
Is my PDF uploaded to a server? +
No. We use pdf-lib, a JavaScript library that runs 100% in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
Are fonts and formatting preserved? +
Yes. pdf-lib copies all pages exactly as they are, including fonts, images, and formatting. The merged PDF looks identical to the originals.
