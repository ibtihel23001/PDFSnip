FAQ

Merge PDF — Common Questions

How many PDF files can I merge? +
There is no hard limit on the number of files. You can merge dozens of PDFs in one go. The total combined size should not exceed 500MB.
Can I reorder pages before merging? +
Yes — after uploading your PDFs, you can drag and drop them to reorder which document comes first, second, etc. Full page-level reordering is coming soon.
Will the merged PDF be searchable? +
Yes. Text content from all merged PDFs is preserved, including embedded fonts and OCR text. The resulting merged PDF is fully searchable.
Are bookmarks and links preserved? +
Internal document bookmarks are preserved where possible. Cross-document links may not work after merging, but page content and formatting are maintained.
