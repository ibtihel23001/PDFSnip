FAQ

Compress PNG — Common Questions

Does PNG compression affect transparency? +
No! Our PNG compressor fully preserves alpha channel transparency. Your transparent backgrounds will remain intact after compression.
What is lossless vs lossy PNG compression? +
Lossless compression reduces file size without changing any pixels — the output is identical to the input. Lossy compression reduces the color palette slightly for much smaller files. For logos and graphics, lossy is often undetectable visually.
How much can PNG files be compressed? +
Lossless compression typically saves 10–30%. Lossy PNG compression can reduce files by 50–80% while maintaining excellent visual quality, especially for images with large flat-color areas.
Will text in PNG images stay sharp? +
Yes. PNG compression does not blur or soften edges. Text, icons, and line art remain pixel-perfect regardless of the compression settings used.
