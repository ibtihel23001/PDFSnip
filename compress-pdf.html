FAQ

Common Questions

How much will my PDF shrink? +
It depends on the PDF. Text-heavy PDFs with lots of metadata can shrink by 20–50%. Image-heavy PDFs may see smaller reductions (5–15%) since images can't be resampled in the browser without converting to another format first.
Will the content look different after compression? +
No. This tool only strips hidden metadata and optimises the PDF container structure. All visible content — text, images, fonts — remains completely unchanged.
Is my PDF uploaded anywhere? +
No. We use pdf-lib which runs entirely in your browser. Your file never leaves your device.
Can I compress multiple PDFs at once? +
Yes! Upload as many PDFs as you need. They are each compressed individually and available for download one by one.
